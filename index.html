How it works

1

Start a chat

Click "Start a new chat". You instantly get two things: an encrypted invite link and a passphrase.

2

Share them separately

Send the link via WhatsApp, email, or any app. Send the passphrase a different way — a phone call, SMS, or say it in person. Keeping them apart means nobody can intercept both.

3

They open the link & reply

The other person opens your link, types the passphrase, and gets a reply link that is automatically copied. They send it back to you.

4

Paste & you're connected

Paste their reply link — you're instantly connected. Everything is end-to-end encrypted. No accounts, no servers, no history. Gone when you close the tab.
